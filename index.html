Hi, I'm Brandon! I'm a library and information science student trying to understand how people look for and use information in their everyday routines.

About

I’m passionate about seeing people succeed. Ultimately, I think people want to feel like they belong and are supported in their goals, whether at work or in life. My mission is to make sure they have access to the tools and information to do just that.

I’m all about getting the right information to the right people in the right place at the right time.

Interests

I have a lot of different areas I’d like to study more, but here are a few major ones:

Information Seeking Behavior

How are people searching for information? Are they successful? What makes their success rate change?

System Design and Evaluation

Is a system working for its users? Is it helping them achieve their goals? Can it be better?

Knowledge Management

How can we record, preserve, and access knowledge that builds over time among many users?

Data Visualization

How can data be presented to users in a way that is accessible and understandable?

Linked Data and Semantic Web

How can we make the web smarter for all users? How can we show relationships between infromation?

Usability and UX Research

How can we test products and systems in a way that benefits users in the long run?
